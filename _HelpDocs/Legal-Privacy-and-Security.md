# 🔒 Privacy & Security

Your health information is deeply personal.  
**Medication Sidekick** is designed with a privacy-first approach, ensuring your medication data stays protected and under your control.

We believe your health data should belong only to **you**.

---

## 🛡 Local Data Storage

All medication records, schedules, and notes you enter into Medication Sidekick are stored **locally on your device**.

This means:

- Your data remains on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac  
- It is not uploaded to external servers owned by us  
- It is never sold or shared with third parties  

Your information stays within the **secure Apple ecosystem**.

---

## ☁️ Optional iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud for Medication Sidekick, your data may synchronize across your Apple devices using **Apple's secure iCloud infrastructure**.

Key points:

- Data sync occurs **through your personal iCloud account**
- Medication Sidekick **cannot access your iCloud account**
- Apple encrypts iCloud data during transfer and storage

You can disable iCloud syncing at any time through your **device settings**.

---

## 🔐 Device Security

Medication Sidekick relies on the built-in security features of your Apple device:

- Face ID or Touch ID protection
- Device passcode encryption
- Apple Secure Enclave hardware
- App sandboxing

These technologies help ensure that your medication information remains protected.

---

## 📡 Network Connections

Medication Sidekick only communicates with external services when required for app functionality that **you choose to enable**.

Examples may include:

- iCloud synchronization (if enabled)
- Apple system services required for normal app operation

The app does **not connect to advertising networks or marketing services**.

---

## 🚫 No Ads. No Tracking.

Medication Sidekick is intentionally designed to be **free from advertising and tracking**.

The app:

- Does not display third-party ads
- Does not include advertising SDKs
- Does not track your behavior
- Does not collect analytics about your medication usage

Your medication history remains **private to you**.

---

## 📤 Sharing Your Data

You maintain full control over how your medication information is shared.

If you choose to share information with a healthcare professional, this is done **manually by you**, such as by:

- Exporting medication records
- Sharing screenshots or reports
- Providing information during a medical appointment

Medication Sidekick **does not automatically transmit your data** to healthcare providers.

---

## 🧭 Our Privacy Commitment

Medication Sidekick was built with the belief that **health apps should respect your privacy by default**.

We commit to:

- Keeping your data under your control
- Avoiding unnecessary data collection
- Being transparent about how the app works

Your medication information belongs to **you — and only you**.
