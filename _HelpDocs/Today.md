The Today screen focuses on what is due now and what you have completed today.

## Quick Steps

1. Open Today in the morning to preview your schedule.
2. Mark doses as completed as you go.
3. Recheck your Daily Status before end of day.

## Daily Status Card

![Image](MS_IPH_TodayView_DailyStatusCard)

## Tip

If something looks off, compare Today with each medication schedule to confirm timing rules.
