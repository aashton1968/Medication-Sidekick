The Settings page lets you control app preferences and behavior.

## Quick Steps

1. Open Settings from the menu.
2. Review each preference section.
3. Update options to match your routine.
4. Return to Home or Today and confirm behavior.

## Settings Overview

![Image](MS_IPH_SetttingsView)

## Common Changes

1. Review preferences after app updates.
2. Recheck settings if reminders do not behave as expected.
3. Confirm your preferred defaults after major edits.
