Use this page for common issues and quick fixes.

## Quick Steps

1. Identify the symptom below.
2. Follow the listed checks in order.
3. Reopen the related app page and confirm the issue is resolved.

## Notifications are not appearing

1. Confirm notification permissions are enabled in iOS settings.
2. Verify schedule times are configured correctly.
3. Reopen the app and check Today and Medications for expected doses.

## Dose times look wrong

1. Review medication schedules.
2. Confirm meal-time settings if schedules depend on meals.
3. Verify device date/time settings.

## Data looks incomplete

1. Check whether the medication has active schedules.
2. Confirm stock and timing details are saved.
3. Reopen relevant views to refresh.
