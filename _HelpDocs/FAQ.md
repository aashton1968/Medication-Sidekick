## Quick Steps

1. Scan the questions below for your issue.
2. Follow linked pages for full instructions.
3. If needed, continue to [Troubleshooting](08-Troubleshooting.md).

## How do I add a medication?

Go to the Medications section and create a new medication entry, then add schedule and stock details.

## How is adherence tracked?

Adherence is based on whether scheduled doses are completed on time.

## Why is my next dose not showing as expected?

Check medication schedules, meal-time dependencies, and any time-based constraints.

## Where can I update meal times?

Open Meal Times and edit the relevant item.

## Where can I change app behavior?

Use Settings to control app-level preferences.
