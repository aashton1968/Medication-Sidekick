# 🔒 **Privacy Policy**

## 🧭 **Introduction**
This app is developed by Ashton IT Consulting.  
Your personal data is important, and the app is designed with a privacy‑first approach to protect your information and your right to privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled when you use the app.

## 📋 **Information We Collect**
Some features may require access to personal or health‑related data, depending on the app’s purpose.

## 🩺 **Health Data**
With your explicit permission, the app may read data from Apple Health (HealthKit).  
HealthKit access is read‑only unless otherwise stated.  
The app does not modify, delete, or overwrite any Apple Health data.  
Health data is used solely to display charts, generate summaries, or support personal record keeping.

## ✍️ **User‑Generated Data**
You may choose to enter information directly into the app, such as measurements, notes, or personal settings.  
This data is stored locally on your device and, if enabled, in your personal iCloud account.

## 🚫 **Information We Do Not Collect**
The app does not collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address, location data, advertising identifiers, analytics, behavioral tracking, or marketing data.

## 🛡 **Data Storage & Processing**
All app data is stored locally on your device.  
Optional iCloud sync uses Apple’s CloudKit and your personal iCloud account.  
Data is never uploaded to third‑party servers operated by the developer.  
We do not sell, rent, or share your data.

## 📡 **Network Connections**
The app connects only to Apple‑provided services when required for functionality, such as:  
- Apple Health (HealthKit)  
- iCloud (CloudKit)  
- App Store services for subscriptions or purchases  
No background tracking, advertising, or analytics connections exist.

## 💳 **Subscriptions & Payments**
All purchases and subscriptions are handled securely by Apple’s App Store.  
Payment information is entered outside the app.  
The developer does not have access to your payment or billing details.  
Purchase eligibility and restoration are managed by Apple.

## 📤 **Data Sharing**
The app does not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, marketing platforms, or analytics providers.  
If you choose to share information with a healthcare provider or another party, this is done manually
