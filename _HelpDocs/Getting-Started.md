This guide helps you get up and running quickly with Medication Sidekick.

## Quick Steps

1. Open the app and review the Home screen.
2. Use the menu to learn where each feature lives.
3. Add your first medication.
4. Confirm your schedule and stock details.
5. Check Today to make sure upcoming doses look correct.

## Home Screen

![Image](MS_IPH_HomeView)

The Home screen shows your next dose, progress, and key shortcuts at a glance.

## Navigation Menu

![Image](MS_IPH_HomeView_Menu)

Use the menu to jump between Home, Today, Medications, Meal Times, and Settings.

## Tip

After your first setup, check the Today page once in the morning and once in the evening to keep your schedule accurate.
